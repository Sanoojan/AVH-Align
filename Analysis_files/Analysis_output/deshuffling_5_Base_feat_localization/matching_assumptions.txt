Audio/visual deshuffling localization assumptions
================================================
1. Zero-shot: no learned model, classifier, checkpoint, or calibration is used.
2. Fake regions are harder to recover when matching audio frames to shuffled visual frames.
3. Audio and visual arrays in each feature .npz are already frame-aligned.
4. Feature-frame labels in *_labels.npz use the same temporal resolution as audio/visual features.
5. Temporal AP/AR proposals interpret feature frames at 25 fps, matching eval_new_time.py.
6. Multiple deshuffling rounds estimate a stable fake score by averaging independent visual shuffles.
7. Video-level scoring emphasizes suspicious spans by averaging the highest-scoring frame fraction.
